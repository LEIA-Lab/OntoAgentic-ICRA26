Invited Talks

Distinguished speakers and their presentations at the ICRA 2026 OntoAgentic Robotics Workshop

Distinguished Speakers

We are honored to host leading researchers and practitioners in the field of OntoAgentic robotics for keynote presentations and panel discussions.

Keynote Speakers

Speaker 1

Professor of Computer Science, MIT

Dr. Smith is a leading researcher in ontological reasoning and multi-agent systems. Her work focuses on developing formal frameworks for knowledge representation in robotic systems and has been published in top-tier venues including IJCAI, AAAI, and ICRA.

Talk Title: "Foundations of OntoAgentic Robotics"

This talk will explore the theoretical foundations of OntoAgentic robotics, examining how ontological reasoning can be integrated with agentic behavior to create more intelligent and adaptive robotic systems. We will discuss current challenges and future research directions in this emerging field.

09:30 - 10:30 60 minutes

Speaker 2

Senior Research Scientist, Google DeepMind

Dr. Johnson specializes in embodied AI and human-robot interaction. His research combines machine learning with robotic systems to enable more natural and effective collaboration between humans and robots in various application domains.

Talk Title: "Future Directions in OntoAgentic Robotics"

This presentation will look ahead to the future of OntoAgentic robotics, discussing emerging trends, technological challenges, and potential applications. We will explore how advances in AI, robotics, and knowledge representation are converging to create new possibilities for intelligent robotic systems.

16:00 - 17:00 60 minutes

Panel Discussion

Panel Topic: "Challenges and Opportunities in OntoAgentic Robotics"

17:00 - 17:30

A moderated discussion featuring all keynote speakers and workshop organizers, focusing on current challenges, research opportunities, and future directions in the field of OntoAgentic robotics.

Moderator

Dr. Sarah Wilson, Workshop Co-Chair

Panelists
  • Dr. Jane Smith, MIT
  • Dr. Michael Johnson, Google DeepMind
  • Dr. Robert Chen, Stanford University
  • Dr. Maria Garcia, Carnegie Mellon University
