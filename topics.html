Topics

Research areas and themes for the ICRA 2026 Workshop on Human-Level Robotic Teammates

Topics

Cognitive Agent Architectures as Orchestrators in Agentic Robotics

  • Cognitive agents as high-level controllers for robotics
  • Orchestration of heterogeneous AI modules (ML + symbolic)
  • Integration of perception, reasoning, planning, and action execution

Neurosymbolic Integration

  • Symbol grounding: linking perceptual data to symbolic representations
  • Hybrid reasoning: combining learned models with knowledge-based inference

Long-Horizon and Hierarchical Planning

  • Planning under uncertainty with commonsense knowledge
  • Multi-step, multi-agent coordination in real-world scenarios
  • Bridging short-term control and long-term goals via cognitive agents

Human-Robot Teaming & Theory of Mind

  • Modeling others' beliefs, goals, and intentions (mindreading)
  • Role allocation and adaptive collaboration in mixed teams
  • Socially aware and explainable interaction strategies

Perception and Action Grounding

  • Mapping sensorimotor signals to symbolic structures
  • Natural language grounding in perception and execution
  • Situated understanding of environments and objects

Knowledge Resources

  • Ontologies, lexicons, and world models for robotics
  • Introspection, self-modeling, and knowledge repair

Learning

  • Learning by understanding through ontological interpretation of percepts
  • Integration of different types of learning

Trust, Explainability, and Ethics

  • Transparent reasoning and communicable decision-making
  • Mechanisms for self-explanation and accountability in agents
  • Designing trustworthy cognitive architectures for robotics

Multi-Modal Integration

  • Combining vision, language, haptics, and proprioception
  • Cross-modal learning and attention mechanisms

Benchmarks and Evaluation

  • Measuring cognitive capabilities in robots
  • Standardized tasks for human-robot collaboration
  • Metrics for trust, explainability, and team performance

Resource Sharing and Infrastructure

  • Promoting platform-independent frameworks for cognitive robotics
  • Open-source tools and shared evaluation platforms
