Detailed schedule for the ICRA 2026 OntoAgentic Robotics Workshop

Workshop Schedule

The workshop will be held as a full-day event featuring paper presentations, poster sessions, and two main discussion panels focused on assessment of current approaches and community-building for future work.

09:00 - 09:30

Welcome & Opening Remarks

Workshop introduction and overview of the day's activities

Organizing Committee
09:30 - 10:30

Paper Session 1: Cognitive Agent Architectures

Presentations on cognitive agents as high-level controllers, orchestration of heterogeneous AI modules, and integration of perception, reasoning, planning, and action execution

09:30 - 09:45 Paper 1: Cognitive Architecture Design
09:45 - 10:00 Paper 2: AI Module Orchestration
10:00 - 10:15 Paper 3: Perception-Reasoning Integration
10:15 - 10:30 Q&A and Discussion
10:30 - 11:00

Coffee Break & Networking

Networking opportunity and refreshments

11:00 - 12:30

Paper Session 2: Neurosymbolic Integration

Presentations on symbol grounding, hybrid reasoning, and integration of learned models with knowledge-based inference

11:00 - 11:15 Paper 4: Symbol Grounding Approaches
11:15 - 11:30 Paper 5: Hybrid Reasoning Systems
11:30 - 11:45 Paper 6: Knowledge-Based Inference
11:45 - 12:00 Paper 7: Perceptual Data Integration
12:00 - 12:30 Q&A and Discussion
12:30 - 14:00

Lunch Break

Lunch and informal networking

14:00 - 15:30

Paper Session 3: Human-Robot Teaming

Presentations on theory of mind, role allocation, and socially aware interaction strategies

14:00 - 14:15 Paper 8: Theory of Mind in Robotics
14:15 - 14:30 Paper 9: Role Allocation Strategies
14:30 - 14:45 Paper 10: Social Interaction Design
14:45 - 15:00 Paper 11: Mixed Team Coordination
15:00 - 15:30 Q&A and Discussion
15:30 - 16:00

Coffee Break & Poster Session

Interactive poster presentations and demonstrations

16:00 - 17:00

Assessment Panel: Current Approaches to Cognitive Robotics

Interactive panel discussion devoted to assessment of the strengths and weaknesses of current approaches to cognitive robotics

Panel Moderator & Community Experts
17:00 - 17:30

Community-Building Panel: Future Work Program

Interactive panel devoted to sketching a program of future work in cognitive robotics and human-robot teaming

All Participants & Organizers

Important Notes

  • All times are in local time zone
  • Presentations should be 10-12 minutes with 3-5 minutes for Q&A
  • Poster presentations will be available throughout the day
  • Lunch and coffee breaks provide networking opportunities
  • Recordings of presentations will be made available after the workshop
